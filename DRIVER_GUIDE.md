# Race Wars Driver Guide

## Getting Started

### Account Setup

1. **Create an Account**
   - Visit app.racewars.local
   - Click "Sign Up"
   - Use email/password or sign in with Google/Apple
   - Verify your email address

2. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Add your display name
   - Set your preferred car number
   - Upload a profile picture (optional)

### Setting Up Your Car Profile

1. **Add Your Car**
   - Navigate to "My Cars" in the settings menu
   - Click "Add New Car"
   - Enter your car details:
     - Make and model
     - Year
     - Color
     - Car class (if applicable)
   - Upload a photo of your car (optional)
   - Set as default if this is your primary car

2. **Car Specifications** (Optional)
   - Add engine details
   - Add performance specifications
   - Add any modifications
   - This information helps with fair classification

## Joining a Race Session

### Finding a Session

1. **Browse Available Sessions**
   - Go to the "Sessions" tab
   - Filter by:
     - Session type (Race, Practice, Qualifying)
     - Track location
     - Car class
     - Date/time

2. **Session Details**
   - Review track information
   - Check session rules and regulations
   - View participating drivers
   - Confirm your car meets requirements

### Joining

1. **Register for Session**
   - Click "Join Session"
   - Select your car profile
   - Confirm your registration
   - You'll receive a confirmation notification

2. **Pre-Session Checklist**
   - Ensure your phone is charged
   - Enable GPS location services
   - Check you have a stable internet connection
   - Mount your phone securely in your car

## During a Race Session

### Starting the Session

1. **Connect to Session**
   - Open the app 5 minutes before start time
   - The session will appear in "My Sessions"
   - Tap "Connect" to join
   - Wait for the session to start

2. **GPS Tracking**
   - The app will automatically start tracking your position
   - Ensure your phone has a clear view of the sky
   - Keep the app open during the session
   - Minimizing the app may affect GPS accuracy

### On-Track Features

1. **Real-Time Positioning**
   - Your position is tracked in real-time
   - View your position on the track map
   - See other drivers' positions
   - Check your lap times

2. **Lap Timing**
   - Automatic lap detection
   - Live lap time display
   - Best lap time tracking
   - Sector split times

3. **Incidents**
   - The system automatically detects:
     - Off-track excursions
     - Crashes
     - Spins
     - Stalls
   - You'll be notified if an incident is detected
   - Review incidents in the session timeline

### Safety Features

1. **Flag System**
   - Yellow flag: Caution, slow down
   - Red flag: Stop immediately
   - Blue flag: Faster car approaching
   - Black flag: Return to pits

2. **Emergency Stop**
   - Tap the emergency button if you need to stop
   - This will notify race control
   - Other drivers will be alerted

## Post-Session

### Reviewing Your Performance

1. **Session Results**
   - View your finishing position
   - Check your lap times
   - Review sector splits
   - Compare with other drivers

2. **Incident Review**
   - View any incidents involving you
   - See penalties (if any)
   - Appeal decisions if needed

### Experience Points

1. **Earning XP**
   - Participate in sessions: +10 XP
   - Complete a session: +20 XP
   - Clean race (no incidents): +30 XP
   - Podium finish: +50 XP

2. **Leveling Up**
   - XP unlocks new features
   - Higher levels may grant special privileges
   - View your progress in your profile

## Troubleshooting

### GPS Issues

**Problem: Poor GPS accuracy**
- Ensure location services are enabled
- Move to an area with better sky visibility
- Avoid being near tall buildings or trees
- Restart the app if needed

**Problem: App not tracking position**
- Check that you've connected to the session
- Verify GPS is enabled
- Ensure the app has location permissions
- Try force-closing and reopening the app

### Connection Issues

**Problem: Lost connection**
- Check your internet connection
- The app will attempt to reconnect automatically
- If connection fails, your position may not be tracked
- Reconnect as soon as possible

**Problem: App crashes**
- Restart the app
- Reconnect to the session
- Your progress will be preserved
- Report the crash if it persists

## Best Practices

### Before the Race

- Charge your phone fully
- Use a phone mount for secure positioning
- Test GPS accuracy before the session
- Familiarize yourself with the track layout
- Review the session rules

### During the Race

- Keep the app open for best GPS accuracy
- Drive safely and follow track rules
- Be aware of other drivers' positions
- Use the map to track your progress
- Monitor flag notifications

### After the Race

- Review your performance data
- Learn from your lap times
- Check for any incidents or penalties
- Provide feedback on the session
- Share your results with friends

## Safety Guidelines

1. **Never use the app while driving**
   - Set up the app before you start driving
   - Mount your phone where you can see it safely
   - Don't interact with the app while in motion

2. **Focus on driving**
   - The app is a tool, not a distraction
   - Your safety is the priority
   - Follow all traffic laws and track rules

3. **Emergency situations**
   - Use the emergency button if needed
   - Pull over safely if you have technical issues
   - Contact race control if you need assistance

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need a special device?**
A: Any smartphone with GPS and internet connection will work.

**Q: How accurate is the GPS tracking?**
A: Typically within 5-10 meters under good conditions.

**Q: Can I use the app offline?**
A: No, an internet connection is required for real-time tracking.

**Q: What happens if my phone dies during a race?**
A: Your data up to that point is saved, but tracking will stop.

**Q: Can I appeal a penalty?**
A: Yes, use the incident review feature to submit an appeal.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes, we protect your data according to our privacy policy.
